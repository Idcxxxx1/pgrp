Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PGRP Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you interact with the Bot within the PGRP Discord server.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide Bot functionality, including:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Vehicle registration details you submit (plate, color, make, model, year)
  • Ticket data related to your support requests
  • Interaction logs to improve Bot performance

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Bot features, including:

  • Storing and displaying vehicle registrations
  • Managing and tracking support tickets
  • Logging commands for troubleshooting

We do not sell or share your data with third parties outside the PGRP community.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using encrypted databases. We implement best practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection where applicable

Please contact the Bot developer or server admin for such requests.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot itself does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Any related Discord or browser tracking is outside the scope of this policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or Bot functionality. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact the Bot developer or PGRP server admins.
